About Us

At HAT UAE, we specialize in the export of high-quality auto parts to clients around the world. With years of industry experience and a commitment to reliability, we serve as a trusted link between manufacturers and international buyers. Our product range covers a wide variety of vehicle makes and models and aftermarket parts for engines, suspension, braking systems, electrical components, and more.

Headquartered in Dubai, we pride ourselves on offering competitive prices, prompt delivery, and personalized customer service. Our dedicated team ensures that every order meets our stringent quality standards and arrives safely and on time, no matter where you are.

Whether you’re a distributor, retailer, or service center, Highland Auto Traders UAE is your dependable partner in the global auto parts supply chain.
